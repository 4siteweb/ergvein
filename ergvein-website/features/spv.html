SPV stands for Simplifed Payment Verification.

Once you create a wallet and choose which currencies you want to add to the wallet, the app connects to indexers, downloads and stores filters for that currency.

Indexers are a part of our project. They are open-sourced as well, so you can set up you own indexer and connect it to the network.
